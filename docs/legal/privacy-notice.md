# KS2 Spelling privacy notice

Effective date: 23 July 2026

KS2 Spelling is a local-first learning application for children, with a
separate Parent-controlled area.

## Data kept on the device

The application stores learner nicknames, year groups, weekly goals, spelling
answers and progress, active practice state, Inklet and Camp progress, Parent
security settings, and any last verified purchase or installed-pack state.
This data is used only to provide the application on that device.

The Parent PIN itself is not stored. The application stores a salted verifier
so it can check a PIN locally. Biometric authentication is performed by the
device operating system; the application does not receive biometric data.

## Network and sharing

KS2 Spelling contains no advertising, analytics or tracking service and does
not create child accounts. Learner profiles, spelling answers, progress,
Inklet and Camp state are not sent to the commerce or download service.

When release commerce is enabled, the application may send a store purchase
proof to the entitlement service and contact the relevant app store to buy,
restore or verify a purchase. It may then download signed spelling-pack data.
Those operations use purchase and pack authority only, not learner data.

A Parent may explicitly export a learning backup through the system share
sheet or document picker. The exported copy is then controlled by the Parent
and by the chosen destination.

## Retention and control

A Parent can reset one learner's learning or delete that learner in the Parent
area. Deleting the application removes its remaining application-controlled
local data. It does not remove backup copies previously exported elsewhere.

## Publication contact

Support and data enquiries use the developer contact published with the
application's store listing. The final store URL, publisher identity and
store-disclosure answers are publication metadata and must be verified during
the final release proof; they are not inferred by this notice.
